## What the ECG shows

- Wellens

---

## Learning points

- Sign of proximal left anterior descending stenosis
- Symmetrical, often deep (>2 mm), T wave inversions in the anterior precordial leads
- A less common variant is biphasic T wave inversions in the same leads
